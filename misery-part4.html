THE MISERY MARKET: PART FOUR

At the edge of collapse, Cal Emmerich makes his final move.

Convinced he has found the Misery Market’s breaking point, he prepares to trigger a synchronised OHC collapse that could erase the old order forever.

But revolutions are never as clean as they sound, and as the clock runs down, Cal’s vision of liberation begins to splinter under the weight of fear, grief, and the people left behind.

In Sector 9, Rick Miller’s fate hangs in the balance inside the detention centre, where one wrong choice could decide whether he survives the coming chaos.

Captain Nia Vollen stands between a creator she can no longer trust and a revolutionary she cannot fully control, while Dr Elias Morran must confront the broken logic of the system he built from pain, loss, and guilt.

As alliances shatter and truths surface, the final reckoning becomes less about destroying the Market than about what remains of the people who tried to end it.

Sci-fi psychological thriller about collapse, reckoning, sacrifice, and what survives after systems fail.
