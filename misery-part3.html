THE MISERY MARKET: PART THREE

As Sector 9 tightens around fear and procedure, Cal Emmerich turns Sector 12 into something far more dangerous than a refuge.

What begins as a hidden alternative to the Misery Market grows into a movement, as Cal draws detainees, dissidents, and the wounded toward a vision of freedom that threatens to upend everything Elias Morran built.

His influence spreads quietly but relentlessly, and with every follower and every carefully planted doubt, he pushes the system closer to a breaking point.

Meanwhile, Captain Nia Vollen returns to Sector 9 and finds herself caught between two forces that both claim to offer salvation.

Elias is waking to the collapse of his own creation, Arsen Vale has been stripped of power, and Rick and the other boys are standing at the edge of choices that could change their lives forever.

But at the centre of it all is Cal, calm and persuasive, increasingly impossible to ignore. A man who seems to understand that revolutions are not won by force alone, but by making people believe they are choosing freedom for themselves.

Sci-fi psychological thriller about rebellion, ideology, control, and the seductive architecture of freedom.
